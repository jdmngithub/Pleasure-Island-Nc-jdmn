    About Us

    About the Pleasure Island Chamber

    The Pleasure Island Chamber of Commerce was established more than 75 years ago as a membership-based, non profit organization. Our Mission is to:

    • Always serve our Members by actively promoting their businesses on and off the Island
    • Enhance the economic vitality of southeast North Carolina through focused leadership and responsible representation
    • Great place to do business
    • Create a community-wide resource for Members to network, exchange ideas and, ultimately, enhance their profitability
    • Serve as Ombudsman for Member businesses in addressing their issues, concerns and needs
    • Help make Pleasure Island, and southeast North Carolina, the best place to Live, Work and Play
